PM‑VIKAS IoT Internship

PM‑VIKAS (Pradhan Mantri Virasat Ka Samvardhan) is a flagship Govt. of India skill-development scheme run by the Ministry of Minority Affairs. Albert was selected as one of 150 participants nationwide for its IoT training track, hosted at IIIT Kottayam — the first higher education institution in India to implement the scheme. Ten days, three phases, one continuous build-up from first principles to a working cloud-connected system.

Phase 1 · Foundations

Week 1
Inauguration of the PM‑VIKAS IoT training program. Introduced to IoT nodes, device-to-cloud architectures, real-world smart systems (cities, grid networks), and communication layers.
Studied Arduino Uno and ESP32 board layouts. Installed the Arduino IDE, configured port selections, wrote basic C script routines, and flashed standard status-LED programs.
Detailed analysis of resistors, capacitors, and LEDs. Hands-on breadboard wiring and simple circuit design rules.
Configured and tested digital/analog GPIO pins. Measured voltage, loop currents, and resistance across wired test circuits with a digital multimeter.

Phase 2 · Sensors & Wireless

Week 2
Interfaced light-dependent resistors to read changing analog light-intensity levels, and programmed thresholds to trigger output alerts on ambient illumination changes.
Interfaced a DHT11 sensor to monitor temperature and humidity, writing data validation logic to filter noise and format values for transmission.
Set up a microcontroller's Wi‑Fi module, established station (STA) connections to local networks, and checked communication protocols — IP addressing, TCP client/server models.

Phase 3 · Cloud & Capstone

Week 3
Created a developer account on Blynk IoT Cloud, set up device templates and virtual datastreams, generated authentication tokens, and configured a mobile dashboard.
Programmed the microcontroller to upload live environmental readings (light intensity, temperature/humidity) to the cloud dashboard, verifying low-latency response and remote app widgets.
Linked the device to a cloud logging service for long-term data visualisation, and assembled the capstone: an integrated smart-monitoring system tracking sensors and controlling output relays.
